Wedding Camera

iOS Swift AWS Open Source

A disposable camera app for weddings. Guests pick their name, choose between a 27-shot disposable or 10-shot Polaroid mode, and shoot. Photos are filtered, saved to Camera Roll, and uploaded silently to a private S3 album — no AWS credentials on device.
